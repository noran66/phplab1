Dishes Description

Burger: A burger is a sandwich consisting of a cooked patty of ground meat, usually beef, placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun. It is often garnished with toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, and condiments like ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise.

Pasta: Pasta is a staple food of Italian cuisine made from unleavened dough consisting of wheat flour, water, and sometimes eggs. It is typically formed into various shapes and sizes, such as spaghetti, penne, and macaroni, and cooked by boiling in water.
